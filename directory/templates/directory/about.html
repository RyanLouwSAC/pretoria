ABOUT

The Pretoria Local is a community-focused business that aims to stimulate the local economy by connecting businesses with local consumers. Through a user-friendly website, we offer an exclusive business directory where local entrepreneurs can list their services and products. We also provide social media promotion services to increase visibility for these businesses, creating a strong online presence and fostering community engagement.

Johandri Johnson - Co Owner.
Co Owner & Social media manager.

OUR VISION

To become the leading platform that fosters a thriving and interconnected local economy in Pretoria, where every business, from small startups to established enterprises, can grow, succeed, and contribute to a vibrant community.

OUR MISSION

Our mission is to support and empower local businesses in Pretoria by providing a dynamic online directory and leveraging social media to promote their products and services. We aim to connect businesses with the local community, driving economic growth and fostering a strong sense of community pride in shopping and supporting local. Through our efforts, we strive to create a sustainable and prosperous environment where every local business has the opportunity to flourish.
